Before You Upload
Remove Student Names

Before scanning or uploading, ensure you cross out, cover, or blur any names or roll numbers written on the question paper to protect student privacy.

Scan and Upload

Use a mobile scanning application (such as Adobe Scan or Microsoft Lens) to capture all pages in clear, high-resolution detail, and compile them into a single PDF file.

Compress Files Under 1 MB

Please compress your scanned PDF files to be under 1 MB using free online compression tools (e.g., ILovePDF or Smallpdf) before submitting.

📋  All submissions are manually reviewed by an administrator before appearing publicly. Low-quality files will be rejected.
