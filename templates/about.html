About the Portal

Meet the team behind PYQ Portal and learn about our mission

Marian College Kuttikkanam

Built by students,
for students.

A free, fast, and always-available archive of previous year question papers — designed to make every student's exam preparation a little easier.

01 — Our Mission

We believe every student deserves access to quality study resources — without barriers, without cost.

The PYQ Portal was born from a simple frustration: previous year question papers were scattered, hard to find, and often unavailable when students needed them most. We built this to change that — a clean, fast, and always-available archive for Marian College students across all departments and semesters.
